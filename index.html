Online drum games to feel the emotion of playing drums on stage at a live concert.


With the virtual drums you can live the experience of a drummer performance on the big stage of a rock music concert.

In music world a live concert is the most thrilling time for every drummer and musician; to get to play music in front of a big audience any drummer spends much time practicing with the drums and rehearsing with his band. The powerful sound of live music, the big concert location and the direct contact with the audience, make every live concert a special experience, like a beautiful game. For beginner drummers, seeing a great drummer play live can be more useful than a hundred music lessons.
Start right now your performance with the live version of virtual drums, fans are waiting for your music! You can play the virtual drums online in real time, with your PC keyboard, playing the keys corrisponding to snare, kick, hi hat, toms and cymbals.
Virtual Drumming is a collection of online drum games, you can also play the virtual drum set of great drummers or build your own custom drums, so you can choose color, sound and expand your set with additional percussion.

Click the keys button from the game menu to display and set the correspondence between the PC keys and the elements of the virtual drum set. You can customize the volume of each drum and cymbal by clicking the sounds button. Sounds and key settings are different for each drum set. Live virtual drums and online drum games all require JavaScript to be enabled.
